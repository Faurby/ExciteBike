How to rent and find a bike?

You can rent an exciteBike by selecting one on our map. You must then confirm your selection to receive a code to access your exciteBike. You have 15 minutes to unlock your exciteBike using the code, at which point the rental timer will start. If 15 minutes pass and you donâ€™t unlock your bike, you lose your reservation.

Please note: You can only rent one exciteBike at a time. If you wish to rent a new one, you must first return your current exciteBike.

How to return a bike?

To return an exciteBike, park it somewhere in Copenhagen and press Return Bike.

Our smart locks will automatically lock it for you.
